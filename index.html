hi_stuff

Hailing from the orange groves of southern California, Boo Radley is a brindle English bulldog of supreme class and curiosity. What he lacks in institutional accreditation, he makes up for in intellectual vitality. Just as happy to spend the wine-drunk evening lazing in his inflatable swimming pool as toiling before a book as thick as his paunch, he boasts research interests of Baudelaire, small-business creameries in the desert southwest, and East-West philosophic overlaps, with a particular emphasis on the permanence of Nietzche's "motley cow."
